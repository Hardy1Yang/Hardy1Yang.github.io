About me

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  1 minutes  |  77 words  |  Hau-Hung Yang

I am a highly motivated graduate with a master’s degree in Psychology from National Taiwan University (NTU, the most prestigious university in Taiwan). I have solid training in statistics and quantitative psychology, and I have five years of research experience. For my master’s thesis, I proposed a generalization of the Robbins-Monro process (an adaptive method applicable to psychophysical experiments for threshold estimation) by incorporating other response variables, such as response time and response confidence, into the process.
